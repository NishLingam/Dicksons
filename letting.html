Lettings

Our residential lettings service is designed with flexibility in mind - to suit each individual client's needs. We combine our established local presence with years of experience providing a personal service, enhanced by bright and welcoming offices, friendly staff, and the latest computer technology.

Services

  • Let Only
  • Let and Rent Collection
  • Full Property Management

We offer the above service options at competitive rates, and we carefully vet prospective tenants with full reference checks.

Benefits

  • Properties handled range from small studios to large executive -style homes
  • Furnished, part furnished and unfurnished properties
  • Professional tenancy agreements and inventories
  • Access to company lets
  • Rent guarantee scheme available
  • Only a small administration charge to tenants

Mangement Service

We appreciate the importance of ensuring our clients' properties are maintained in good condition, for the benefit of landlords and tenants alike. To achieve this we undertake regular scheduled inspections of each managed property. Prompt maintenance and repairs are organized at competitive cost from our pool of reliable and experienced contractors. We administer monthly rent collections and forward payments promptly to our landlords with a clear and detailed statement. Our property management service is designed to ensure our landlords are not unduly bothered save for authorization of repairs above an agreed cost level, and that our tenants are able to enjoy the property without hassle or discomfort.
