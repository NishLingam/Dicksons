What will it cost to You..?

We will not normally charge you anything for our services as we usually receive an introduction payment from the chosen lender and/or Legal & General in respect of any life assurance you take connected with the mortgage. This would be similar to the amount received by the lender if you went direct. Therefore there is no additional cost to you whatsoever for our independent mortgage advice service.
